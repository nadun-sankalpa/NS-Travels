Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our vehicle rental service

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

To rent a vehicle, you'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in the renter's name, and proof of insurance. International renters may need an International Driving Permit along with their national driver's license. Age requirements vary by vehicle type, but generally, renters must be at least 21 years old.

Is there a mileage limit on rentals?

Most of our standard rentals come with unlimited mileage within the country of rental. However, some specialty and luxury vehicles may have daily mileage limits. Any restrictions will be clearly stated during the booking process. Additional charges apply if you exceed the mileage limit.

What is your cancellation policy?

For standard reservations, cancellations made at least 48 hours before the pickup time are free of charge. Cancellations made within 48 hours of pickup may incur a fee equivalent to one day's rental. No-shows are charged the full rental amount. Special events and peak season rentals may have different cancellation policies.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between many of our locations. One-way fees may apply depending on the pickup and drop-off locations. These fees vary based on distance and vehicle type. Please specify your one-way rental needs during the booking process to get accurate pricing.

What insurance options are available?

We offer several insurance options including Collision Damage Waiver (CDW), Supplemental Liability Insurance (SLI), Personal Accident Insurance (PAI), and Roadside Assistance Protection (RAP). Your personal auto insurance or credit card may provide coverage for rental vehicles, so check before purchasing additional insurance.
